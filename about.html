Yaread Habte

Occupation: Student at Humber College

Hello! My name is Yaread Habte. I hope you enjoyed looking at my portfolio. For many years, I tried to make sure I was one of the best artists to ever exist. I've been drawing since I was young and I wanted to evolve and get better with my work. Around grade twelve, I first started to use Adobe Photoshop CS6. It was tricky, but I managed to spend enough time to understand the tools provided. After high school, I pursed in Art Foundations at Humber College and that's when I started to learn more about one point and two point perspective. I also learned how to use Adobe Illustrator for the first time and it wasn't easy, despite being similar to Adobe Photoshop. After Art Foundations, I pursued in 3D Animation at Humber College, but realized this program was not for me after spending a few years. So I switched to Design Foundation and learned even more about perspective. I also learned more about Adobe Photoshop and at one point made a Victorian House from scratch. After graduating from Design Foundation, I went into Graphic Design and just like that, this was the perfect program for me to pursue. I am currently in my first year in the program.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact me via email (see contact section). Thank you.
